Welcome to Quail!

Quail connects small businesses with micro-investments and social media marketing services
to provide a return on investment for both micro-investors and small businesses.

Introducing the ultimate microinvesting app
for connecting with small businesses near you

Here's how it works!

Social Empowerment

We create a sense of empowerment for investors who feel connected to a local business, and would like to help them succeed, all while making a return on investment.

Investment Opportunities

We enable young investors to get involved with micro-investing through small businesses, providing them with a monetary return with a feeling of social responsibility.

Marketing Innovation

We augment social media marketing endeavors by educating small businesses through digital marketing agencies to get the maximum value out of their capital.

Strengthening Communities

We promote social entrepreneurship by directly connecting small businesses to micro-investors in a time of fading innovation, ultimately bolstering communities.
